How to Use Location Alarm

Here are the steps to use Location Alarm:

  1. Step 1: Open the Location Alarm app.
  2. Step 2: Set your target location by entering latitude and longitude.
  3. Step 3: Click "Set Alarm" to activate the alarm.
  4. Step 4: When you are near the target location, the alarm will ring.

Benefits and Merits

1. Convenient and easy-to-use location-based alarm system.

2. Helps you avoid missing important locations and events.

3. Customizable settings for alarm radius and notification.
